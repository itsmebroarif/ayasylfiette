How to Order

1

Choose Service & Contact

Check the price list in the Product section. Contact via Form / Email / VGen to discuss concepts and image references.

2

Sketch & Payment

I will provide a rough sketch. If you agree, make a full payment or a 50% down payment to start the process (Lineart & Base Color).

3

Revision & Finalization

Minor revisions are allowed at the base color stage. After rendering is complete, High-Res files (PNG/JPG/PSD) will be sent.

FAQ よくある質問

Can I request pose revisions?

Pose revisions are only allowed during the ROUGH SKETCH stage. If it has reached the lineart or coloring stage, additional charges will apply.

Can you draw Mecha or complex Armor?

For now, my main focus is Anime style, VTuber, and Furry. I might decline complex full-body Armor/Mecha or apply a heavy charge depending on the difficulty.

What are the payment methods?

I accept local Bank Transfers (BCA/Mandiri), E-Wallets (Gopay/OVO/Dana), and payments via the VGen platform for international clients.
